Before/after entry points

If you want to execute some code before or after the form is send, you can use the data-lugajax-before and data-lugajax-after custom attributes

before/after

  1. <script>
  2. handlers = {};
  3. handlers.alertBefore = function(formNode){
  4. alert("I am executed before the form is send");
  5. };
  6. handlers.alertAfter = function(formNode){
  7. alert("I am executed after the form is send");
  8. };
  1. <form action="../action.json" data-lugajax-form="true" data-lugajax-after="handlers.alertAfter" data-lugajax-before="handlers.alertBefore">
